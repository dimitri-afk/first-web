LEOPARD-2A7 (MBT)

The Leopard 2A7 is a modern main battle tank that combines powerful firepower, advanced protection, and excellent mobility. Armed with a 120mm smoothbore gun and supported by sophisticated fire control systems, it delivers precise performance in all combat conditions. Its modular armor and integrated protection systems ensure high survivability against various threats, while a strong engine and advanced suspension provide smooth maneuverability across diverse terrains. Designed with digital systems and future upgrade potential, the Leopard 2A7 is a reliable long-term defense investment. Estimated unit cost: €13–15 million (around $14–16 million USD).

PUMA (IFV)

The Puma Infantry Fighting Vehicle (IFV) is a highly advanced armored platform built to provide superior protection, firepower, and mobility for mechanized infantry units. Developed by Krauss-Maffei Wegmann and Rheinmetall, it features modular AMAP composite armor with scalable protection, a 30mm MK 30-2/ABM autocannon, secondary machine guns, and Spike LR anti-tank guided missiles. Its advanced digital systems, 360° camera coverage, and networking capabilities ensure excellent situational awareness and seamless coordination with dismounted troops. With strong survivability, agility, and future upgrade potential, the Puma IFV stands as one of the most capable vehicles in its class. Estimated unit cost: €21–30 million (around $22–32 million USD).
